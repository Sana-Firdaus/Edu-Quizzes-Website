About Us

After completion of test we will give marks and provide how many questions they have attempted right and wrong.

We also provide material for their knowledge along with motivational video to boost their morale.

Need and Significance

Quizzes can be given without an instructor, which saves money and time. Timer can build pressure on students to attend questions in specified period of time,which will be helpful for practicing time management.

Quizzes raise confidence, well targeted,quizzes can boost self esteem and confidence. When students can challenge themselves but are able to succeed, their confidence can grow.

With instant result of online quiz students can check their progress and continue with their further learning.

Time management ,Planning, Further Knowledge are basic requirement of student which students can attain from attending quiz.
